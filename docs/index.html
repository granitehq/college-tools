Frequently Asked Questions

Is College Tools really free?

Yes, College Tools is currently completely free to use. You just need a Google account to copy the spreadsheet template.

Do I need programming knowledge?

No programming knowledge is required. College Tools works through simple menu commands in Google Sheets.

How do I get a College Scorecard API key?

Visit api.data.gov/signup to get your free API key. It takes less than a minute and requires only your email address. See full setup guide

Is my college data secure?

Yes, all your data stays in your own Google Sheets document. College Tools doesn't store any of your information on external servers.
